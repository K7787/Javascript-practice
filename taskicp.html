Vice Chancellor's Message

Welcome to Islamia College Peshawar, a prestigious institution deeply rooted in the rich heritage of knowledge and academic excellence since its inception in 1913. As a Vice Chancellor, I am immensely proud to lead this esteemed institution, which has been a beacon of enlightenment for over a century. At Islamia College Peshawar, we are committed to nurturing the intellectual, moral, and professional growth of our students, preparing them to become leaders and contributors to society.
